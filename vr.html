Astrology Wheel

Interactive VR Experience in Unity


A virtual reality world in Unity which leads users through a narrative experience. Users navigate through 12 separate environments via a unique controller mechanic and unconventional interaction styles. Experience with animation, triggers, particle systems, etc.

Last year, I took a VR scripting class at Berkeley City College. For the first time, I was introduced to coding for games and Unity. For my final, I created a world in which a character is lead through a story via the mechanic of a spinning wheel that is turned to progress the narrative forward. I was new to modeling, so my first 3D model was the wheel itself, but the rest of the project is compiled from free 3D assets. I later learned to love 3D modeling. Showing people this project, I have been consistently impressed by how amazing VR is to people. I began to understand how the immediacy of the environment could be beneficial to many applications, including education and therapy. With these features in mind, I have been spending this ensuing year learning what I need to enact a program that has a more complex mechanic, hoping to use machine learning as a way to automate and make some processes more unique.
