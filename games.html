Comrades and Cutthroats

Diplomatic Land Aquisition Board Game

Designed a functional board game along with a group of three fellow students. Personally prototyped over 150 individual cards that allowed for three unique play styles. The game explores several ways in which social uprisings and rebellion can be demonstrated through the mechanics of a simple diplomatic land acquisition game. Play testing lead to breakthroughs in the basics of player balance and what drives a participant to engage in different modes of play and the rewards involved.
