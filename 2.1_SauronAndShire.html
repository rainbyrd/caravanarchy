Sauron and The Shire

There exists evil in this world and we should not hide from that fact and try to ignore the rate and methods of its growth.

The evil that exists in this world will not destroy itself. It must be interfaced with and we must create social and economic structures capable of protection from tyranny through self-preparedness, coordination with allies, networking and resource exchange, etc. We need to build a safety net consisting of a decentralized network.
