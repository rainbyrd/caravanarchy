Trip

2019

Lovers! Adventure!

In August, 2019, two sweet babes went on a trip that would change their lives forever. There was camping. There were little, tiny squirrels. And, best of all, they each had a bright, shining star at their side to keep them amazed.

Things I loved about our trip:

  • Being lovers
  • Getting our camping game strong
  • Compromising
  • Good food
  • Spending quality time with people
  • Cuddling


Gig Harbor

We started off out trip in Gig Harbor with the fam. After a harrowing 12 hour journey, Kelly joined us and it was super sweet. The next morning he became a beautiful french-braid bape. Over the next two days, we went on walks, found a blackberry grove, met a hair-monster (really scary) and went to TWO whole family parties.
