Pragmatism: Contractual Language, Islands Of Truth

The idea that we hold the best possible model for our lives at each moment. We must be able to reassess our conclusions, as well as our methods for reaching conclusions, without losing the thread of reality or the ability to learn and grow from experience.

Pragmatism creates temporary islands of truth for one to jump between. It prescribes each individual to use their own judgment to make conclusions, each with its own necessitation.

It makes sense to start one’s philosophy from the assumption that this world is not a fake, projected “matrix” to be escaped from, but instead, that the best course of action is to assume that the world is something we can facilitate in taking its best possible form. The current limitations will be transcended eventually, and with enough concerted effort in a positive direction, that eventuality should be able to come pretty soon.

Pragmatism means there are islands of truth. Though we might not have the perfect grasp upon them, there are still real things/temporary facts that can be temporarily agreed upon and used as temporary variables in other discussions. The final truth to any one question does not need to be attained for there to be an agreed-upon temporary definition of one thing or another. If we use logical axioms and never stray into suppositions, there can still be a contractual language that makes sense.

You Own Your Self

You own yourself and therefore there is no way anyone can have authority over you or what you own unless you have given fully informed consent, which can be revoked at any time.

Self-ownership is one of the basic axiom/tenets of the logic we live by. We have found no evidence to counteract this tenet and thus we hold it as a very secure fact. Every individual reading these words is the owner of themselves and, as an extension of themselves, the owner of their time, intellect, labor, and the products thereof.

You own your mind through homesteading your body because your body is your home, and you stead it. You exclusively home-stead your mind and body and thus you possess the highest claim to exclude others from the use of yourself. You are responsible for and act in good service and upkeep toward your body and mind more than anyone else does or could.

Property

Self-ownership extends to property. There are distinctions within many philosophies that lead to the concept of “sticky property” which dictates the abandonment conditions or special use cases for certain types of property such as land.

Property is a strategy aimed at the reduction of conflict over scarce resources. It is the concept that an individual or group of individuals can decide the proper state of a particular thing, such as but not limited to who may use it, under what terms, etc.

The Infrastructure for Freedom

Hundreds of thousands of people throughout the world are committed to attaining higher levels of freedom for themselves, and many are also passionate about the freedom of others. We already have the infrastructure necessary to sustain freedom for more and more people right now. The thing that has been lacking in other plans we see is a simple and wide-scale solution. The clear, ideologically simple idea of freedom for all people without adherence to one system or another. The means to transition from a state of surviving to one of thriving. The Network is here to bring the coordination necessary to completely defund the systems we do not consent to.

Our model does not require capital to run. It is a framework that can be applied but helps others better utilize what they have. We do not need donations nearly as much as we need people on the ground, explaining what is possible and available to us right now and also creating the next level of possibilities.
